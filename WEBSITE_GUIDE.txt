WHAT IS AURA AI?
----------------
AURA AI is an AI-powered banking complaint management platform that uses
Google Gemini to automatically analyze, categorize, prioritize, and draft
responses for customer complaints — saving bank staff hours of manual work.

DEMO LOGIN CREDENTIALS
-----------------------
  Admin :  admin@aura.ai  /  admin123
  Customer: Register a new account on the login page


================================================================================
  SECTION 1 — HOW TO USE AS ADMIN
================================================================================

STEP 1: Log In
--------------
  - Open the app and enter: admin@aura.ai / admin123
  - The system detects your Admin role and unlocks all admin-only features.

STEP 2: Admin Intelligence Console (Dashboard)
----------------------------------------------
  - 4 gradient stat cards at the top:
      • Total Complaints  (indigo)   — all complaints ever submitted
      • Pending Resolution (amber)   — complaints awaiting action
      • Resolved Cases    (green)    — completed complaints
      • Resolution Rate   (pink)     — % of resolved vs total
  - 7-Day Complaint Volume chart — LIVE data from MongoDB (last 7 days)
  - Category Breakdown pie chart — each category color-coded with progress bars
  - Recent Submissions table — last 5 complaints with priority badges

STEP 3: Manage Complaints (/complaints)
----------------------------------------
  - SEARCH: Type in the search bar to filter by complaint text or category
  - FILTER: Click All / High / Medium / Low to filter by priority level
  - EXPAND: Click the ▾ chevron to see the AI-generated suggested response
  - RESOLVE: Click the ✓ shield icon to mark a complaint as resolved
  - DELETE: Click the 🗑 trash icon to permanently delete a complaint
            (confirmation dialog appears first)
  - EXPORT: Click "Export PDF" to print/save the current view

STEP 4: Analytics Page (/analytics)
-------------------------------------
  - Resolution Rate card with animated progress bar
  - Resolved and Pending count cards
  - Bar chart: complaints by category (live data)
  - Donut chart: sentiment distribution (Positive/Neutral/Negative/Urgent)

STEP 5: Alerts Page (/alerts)
-------------------------------
  - System status banners (operational notices)
  - Live list of HIGH-PRIORITY and URGENT-SENTIMENT complaints
    that are still PENDING — your critical action queue
  - Shows count badge of open urgent items

STEP 6: AI Assistant (/ai)
---------------------------
  - Full chat interface powered by Google Gemini
  - Has LIVE access to your dashboard data before each response
  - Suggested prompts available on the right sidebar:
      • "Summarize today's complaint trends"
      • "Which category has the highest volume?"
      • "How many high-priority issues are unresolved?"
      • "Give me insights on customer sentiment"
  - Copy any AI response using the copy button
  - Clear chat with the reset button at the top right

STEP 7: Guide & Notes (/notes)
--------------------------------
  - Tabbed documentation page with 4 sections:
      Overview | Admin Guide | Customer Guide | Features

STEP 8: Settings (/settings)
------------------------------
  - Toggle dark mode on/off (persists to localStorage)

ADMIN-ONLY FEATURES:
  Analytics Page, Alerts Page, Resolve Button, Delete Button,
  System-wide Stats, 7-Day Trend Chart, All Users' Complaints,
  Full AI context data in Assistant


================================================================================
  SECTION 2 — HOW TO USE AS CUSTOMER
================================================================================

STEP 1: Register an Account
-----------------------------
  - On the Login page, click "Register now"
  - Enter: Full Name, Email, Password
  - Your account is created as Customer role automatically

STEP 2: Customer Dashboard (/)
--------------------------------
  - Welcome banner with your first name
  - 3 stat cards: Total Requests, Pending, Resolved
  - Recent Submissions list (last 4 complaints)
  - "How It Works" step-by-step guide panel
  - "New Complaint" button to jump to the form

STEP 3: Submit a Complaint (/complaints)
-----------------------------------------
  - The complaint form is on the right side of the page
  - Type your issue (up to 500 characters — live counter shown)
  - OR click the 🎤 Mic button for voice input
  - Click "Analyze & Submit"
  - AI processes in 2-5 seconds and shows results:
      • Category  (Fraud / Loan / Account / Card / Other)
      • Sentiment (Positive / Neutral / Negative / Urgent)
      • Priority  (High=red / Medium=orange / Low=blue)
      • AI Suggested Response (what the bank will tell you)

STEP 4: Track Your Complaints
-------------------------------
  - All your complaints appear in the table on the left
  - Filter by priority: All / High / Medium / Low
  - Search by text or category using the search bar
  - Click ▾ chevron on any row to see the AI suggested response
  - Status dot:  Pulsing orange = Pending  |  Solid green = Resolved

STEP 5: AI Assistant (/ai)
---------------------------
  - Ask the AI about your specific complaints
  - The AI has live access to YOUR complaint data
  - Try asking:
      • "What is the status of my complaints?"
      • "How long does it take to resolve a Fraud complaint?"
      • "What happens after I submit a complaint?"
      • "I have an urgent banking issue — what should I do?"

CUSTOMER TIPS:
  ✓ Be specific — include dates, account details, exact issue description
  ✓ Check your dashboard daily for status updates
  ✓ Read the AI Suggested Response to know what reply to expect
  ✓ Use voice input if you're on mobile or can't type easily

================================================================================
  SECTION 5 — BACKEND API ENDPOINTS
================================================================================

  Auth:
    POST /api/users          → Register new user
    POST /api/users/login    → Login, returns JWT token

  Complaints:
    GET    /api/complaints       → Get complaints (own or all if admin)
    POST   /api/complaints       → Submit new complaint (AI-analyzed)
    GET    /api/complaints/stats → Admin: total/pending/resolved/byCategory
    GET    /api/complaints/trends→ Admin: 7-day daily count array
    PUT    /api/complaints/:id   → Admin: update status
    DELETE /api/complaints/:id   → Admin: delete complaint

  AI:
    POST /api/ai/chat            → Chat with AI (injects live context)
